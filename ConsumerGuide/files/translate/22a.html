The Mudroom
If your mudroom looks like a scene from a sci-fi movie where the humans have been
vaporized and all that remains is a scatter of coats, shoes, and bags… then you’re like
just about every other family! Coming home to a pile of ‘stuff’ at the door is frustrating,
not to mention trying to get out that door in the morning with everything everyone needs.

But it doesn’t have to be that way. The family ‘locker room’ has been growing in populari-
ty for one important reason – it really works! Creating an attractive, practical, and
easy-to-use space to store your daily use items will engage your whole family in the art
of being organized.

Our storage design team guides you through the process of creating the ideal mudroom
storage system for your family, incorporating materials and finishes which complement
your interior décor.

Individual lockers allow you easy access to the items you
need, right where you need them to be.

Multiple hooks per locker offer hanging options for jackets,
umbrellas, back-packs, hats, your dog’s leash, your
daughter’s helmet – anything you want to be able to grab
and go.

Overhead cupboards allow you to tuck away off-season
items, while keeping them sorted for each family member.

A built-in bench offers a comfortable place to sit and tie
shoes. Add drawers and recessed shelves below the bench to
easily store extra footwear.
