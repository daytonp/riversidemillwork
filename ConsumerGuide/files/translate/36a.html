The French door
was originally developed
as a way to allow natural light to travel through to
the interior of the home. Whether used to sepa-
rate a formal dining area, highlight the entrance to
a library, or allow visibility from a home office,
French doors will not only serve their original
purpose, but also become a focal point of beauty
in your home. French doors can be used for single
or double openings, and are available in both paint
grade and stain grade wood species.
